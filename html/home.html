Our Medical Services & Specialties

Ultra Sonography (USG)

Uses ultrasound waves to visualize organs, detect abnormalities, monitor pregnancy, guide procedures, and support safe, accurate, real-time medical imaging.

Blood Grouping

Identifies a person's ABO and Rh blood type to ensure safe transfusions, prevent reactions, and support essential medical procedures accurately.

Blood Sugar Test

Measures glucose levels in blood to diagnose diabetes, track treatment effectiveness, evaluate metabolism, and assess overall health conditions accurately.
